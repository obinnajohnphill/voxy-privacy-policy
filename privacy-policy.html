Data Collection and Use

Voxy is designed to facilitate speech recognition and translation. We respect your privacy and ensure that no personal or sensitive data is collected or stored by our app unless explicitly stated.

What Data Do We Collect?

  • Audio Data: Temporarily processed during speech recognition and translation. This data is not stored or shared.
  • Device Permissions: Voxy requires microphone access to enable speech recognition. No additional device information is collected or stored.

How Is Data Used?

Any data processed during the app's operation is used solely for providing the app's core functionality, such as recognising speech and translating it. No data is saved, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or sensitive data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Voxy does not store any user data on our servers or locally on your device. Any data processed during app usage is ephemeral and deleted immediately after use.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard measures to ensure the secure handling of all data processed by Voxy.
